Description ​

Fairytale stepmothers are notoriously wicked. But after a children’s clothing designer is reborn as young Princess Blanche’s stepmom, she’s determined to break the mold and shower her daughter with love. Now she just has to convince the girl’s cold-hearted father to do the same. In her quest to make a happier home for Blanche, Abigail begins to uncover secrets and schemes within the palace. Will devious forces tear the Freidkins apart, or can Abigail stitch them back into a family?

Why you should read this? ​

This comic tells you about a reincarnation story of a middle-aged woman who died and reincarnated into the story of Snow White as the evil step-mother Abigail, and is now smitten by how cute her step-daughter Blanche is.

This story basically like another type of Lucia but in here we won't see a s*x crazed black haired, red eyed Duke, instead a black haired king that's living his life with just fulfilling his duties without having to enjoy life and emotions.

With that sentence alone you probably already considering to start reading this right? Well if you still doubting I have to say that the story itself has a settings and plot that makes sense. A lot of things that happen in this story are justified and nothing happens for no reason. After the FL transmigrated into the original woman's body, things didn't magically change for her. The ML didn't just fall for her and until now some are still doubtful of the FL's drastic change which makes so much sense. The FL is smart, but she's not ruthless and scheming. Her thoughts are realistic and understandable. She knows when to push for her decisions and when to yield.
